About
Mridaksh is the personality contest of Antaragni. It is the single most coveted individual event of
the festival, a fun filled path to the most sought after title of Ms. and Mr. Antaragni. One
can only imagine the battle of wits that ensues right from the initial stages where
puzzling and questionnaires are distributed, all the way to thejam packed finals
in the auditorium with participants showcasing their with, confidence,
skill and talent.
Structure
Your Journey towards being Mr. & Ms. Antaragni
Mridaksh Form

Fill in and submit the Mridaksh questionnaire Online or Offline to register for the event. Based on your creativity,intelligence, wit and common sense 20 entries will be shortlisted for the next round. The form can be filled on the Antaragni website or collected from the Mridaksh desk during Antaragni.

Interview Round

This round is to test your confidence and spontaneity. A panel of interviewers will pose certain questions of you based on your form. We are not looking for mastery of talent or eloquence, but the uniqueness, attitude and energy that you bring to the stage.

Final Rounds

The games end here. A packed auditorium, hanging on to your every word. The stage is all set, just go out there and do what you do best entertain. Four rounds separate you and the title of Mr./Ms. Antaragni.

1.Introduction and Flair Round

Participants will introduce themselves and proceed to showcase their talents on the stage. You need not be a maestro, your confidence and ability to engage the audience is what matters. Your talent doesn't need to be a conventional one either- you can not only sing, dance or play an instrument, but can also recite prose or poetry, present an act, mimicry or even a martial arts performance whatever you need to do to convey to the audience what you're all about!

2.Compere's Round

Each participant get five minutes to "duel". It's one-on-one, you against the comperes. There is only one (unsaid) rule, the compere always wins. What matters is how much of a fight you put up. The questions in this round will be based on what you fill up in your forms, some background information, and whatever else we manage to dig up.

3.Extempore Round

Here main center of concentration will be the instinct and the spontaneity of the participants. Participant will be thrown in a situation where he/she will have to find a solution. They may be asked to continue an act of video clip, or given an act involving on-hand improvisation using a set of props. This round may even involve participants working in pairs to complete the challenge.

4.Judge's round

You have wowed the audience, the comperes, all that is left is to charm the judges and walk away with the title of Ms./ Mr. Antaragni. The judges will have a free hand for this round. They can ask anything and everything that suits their whims.
