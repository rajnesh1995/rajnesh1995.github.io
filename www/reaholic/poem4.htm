I think about you
I think about you
BY SUBHAM GOYAL
September 1, 2014
I think about you
In the morning
hoping that you
are charming

I think about you
in the afternoon
hoping that I
can meet you soon

I think about you
in the evening
hoping that you


I think about you
in the night
hoping that you
understand my plight

I think about you
everytime
hoping that you
are also missing me

You're the first thing I think of when I rise.
You're the last thing I think of when I close my eyes.
You're a person who is so true and kind.
