Why does 'CPI' go down?
Why does 'CPI' go down?
BY SUBHAM GOYAL
27 February 2014
I know many of you are thinking why I'm asking this funny question. And answer of this question coming in your mind is simply due to students do not study.

You all are right if you thought that. Congratulation!!

Seniors asked me and my friends about how much CPI we want to score in first semester. Many of us said we want to score CPI above 8. But this becomes impossible for us after first mid. semester was over. It is due to involving in Extra co-curricular activities before mid Semester. We didn't know how to manage time. We watch new things every day and getting involved in it.

When senior asked the reason for getting low CPI to one of my friends. I remember he said this is due to I thought I don't have to do study much as I did for cracking JEE.

But this is not the only reason behind it. Here, in IIT students get a high speed wlan connection due to which students start using Wifi in class. Some of the students attend classes just to use Wifi. Perhaps they become addicted to using social networking sites like Facebook.

Students who do study regularly and still get less CPI

As some Professors teach fast, students don't understand what Professor teaches. There is a lack of concentration in students. Other reasons are related to personal issues, such as Exam anxiety. Some have family issues, pressure from the parents. Students fear to discuss their problems with the faculty.

CPI less than 6 affect someones future

CPI less than 6?? The first reason is in IIT you are not eligible for any nominations. Another reason you are not eligible for any government job, directly. You won't be short listed for nice companies.You will curse yourself a lot.

Steps should Students take to get Good CPI

  • Attend Classes and concentrate (Make sure that you leave without a single doubt)
  • Study regularly. Do not wait for midsems and endsems to open your books
  • Understand the concepts rather than focusing only on the problems.
  • Enjoy with your friends/wingies (Yes, it is important to perform well)
  • Respect your teachers
  • Quit Facebook and start facing your books.
